<b>Lesson Number 11 "How old are you?"</b>

In this lesson you will learn following expression:

Asking someone their age, and being able to respond.

ⲉⲕϧⲉⲛ ⲟⲩ̣ⲏⲣ ̀ⲛⲣⲟⲙⲡⲓ?
Literal translation: You are in how many of age/years?

ϯϧⲉⲛ 28 ̀ⲛⲣⲟⲙⲡⲓ.
Literal translation: I am in (the) 28 (th) of age/years.

Don't worry about learning to count perfectly yet.
