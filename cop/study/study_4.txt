<bold>Lesson Number 4 "Letters"

In this lesson you will learn the following letters:
̣ⲃ, ⲃ, ϫ, ϧ, ⲗ, ⲭ


The following is a rough guide to the pronunciation:

̣ⲃ^§: "Ⲃ", this letter is always at the
end of a word.

ⲃ^§: "U", in Arabic it’s "و"

ϫ^§: Soft as in English “George”

ϧ^§: Arabic “خ”, “kh” guttural

Ⲗ^§: It is an English “L”

ⲭ^§:"ch” as in English “Choir”
