<bold>Lesson Number 12 "Male Possession"

In this lesson you will how to express your possessions.

Lesson objectives:

The general rule for male possession (talking to a male about his possessions) is, at the end of a noun you add one letter and a vowel "كَ" [ak].

There are exceptions to this rule, for example your brother is أخوك [akhok]
