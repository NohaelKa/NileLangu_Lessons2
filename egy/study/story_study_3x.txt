<b>Lesson Number 2 "The Present Tense"</b>

In this lesson you will learn about using commonly used verbs in the present tense.

The rule is, if the verb in the present tense, it is always ب + verb,
This rule is not in Fus-ha Arabic.

Full list of things to learn:
باكل
بشتغل
بشرب
بطبخ
بعمل رياضة
بلعب درامز
بنضف
بنام
بعمل
