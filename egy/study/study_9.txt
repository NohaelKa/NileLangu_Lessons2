<bold>Lesson Number 9 "Greetings"

In this lesson you will learn common greetings.

Lesson objectives:

Good Morning:
Morning of Goodness [sabaH elkheir]: ‎§صباح الخير^§
Morning of light (response) [sabaH elnoor]: ‎§صباح النور^§

Good Evening:
Evening of Goodness [masa' elkheir]: ‎§مساء الخير^§
Evening of light [masa' elnoor]: ‎§مساء النور^§

Bye:
Peace [salam]: ‎§سلام^§

Time of day:
(In) the morning [elsob7]: ‎§الصبح^§
(In) the afternoon [baed eldohr]: ‎§بعد الظهر^§
(In) the night [beleil]: ‎§بليل^§

Hello and Welcome:
Hello / Welcome/ (Nice to meet you) [ahlan wa sahlan]: ‎§أهلاً و سهلاً^§
Go ahead (to male) (When offering food or offering entry to home) [etfadal]: ‎§إتفضل^§
Go ahead (to female) (When offering food or offering entry to home) [etfadali]: ‎§إتفضلي^§
Go ahead (to plural) (When offering food or offering entry to home) [etfadalu]: ‎§إتفضلو^§

Honored to know you (to male) [etsharaft bmae-rftak]: ‎§إتشرفت بمعرفتَك^§
Honored to know you  (to female) [etsharaft bmae-rftek]: ‎§إتشرفت بمعرفتِك^§
The honor is mine (response) [elsharaf leya]: ‎§الشرف ليا^§
