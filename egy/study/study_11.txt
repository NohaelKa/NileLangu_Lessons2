<bold>Lesson Number 11 "Speaker Possession"

In this lesson you will how to express your possessions.

Lesson objectives:

The rule is simple, at the end of a noun you add one letter "ي". This indicates it is the speakers possession.
It does not matter if the speak is female or male.
This lesson will also teach you new useful nouns.
